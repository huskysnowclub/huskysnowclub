Why the name change?


Yeah, we used to go by the Husky Snowboard Team! We're still the same old group, but since we've been happily accepting skiers for years now (some of our best riders and socialites ride on two boards!) and we're open to all skill levels and non competitive, we thought it would be best to change the "Snowboard Team" part of the name to a more accurate "Snow Club". Rest assured we still ride, ski, and party just as hard as before the name change.


  About Snow Club

We're a club at the University of Washington aimed at increasing involvement in snow sports from the student population. We put on a variety of snowsports related events and run trips while operating a rideshare program to enable anyone an opportunity to go to the mountain.

Benefits of joining the Snow Club

  • Free entry to all regular snowboard team events
  • Access to private members rideshare program
  • Exclusive swag and deal from our sponsors
  • Access to all snowboard team trips and parties

How it works

First, you join the Snow Club. This costs $30, and is a once a year fee, not once a quarter. After you've joined you will get access to our private rideshare area. There drivers can post where you're going and how many spots you have. People who don't drive can communicate with you to fill up your car. While you're on your trip, take a selfie with everyone in the car and bring a gas receipt to our office hours (posted in the private area and on our calendar below) and we'll reimburse you- as long as you've taken up three members. Feel free to contact the officers or stop by at any of our events to ask any questions.
